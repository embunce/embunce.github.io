Introduction

Bunce Bikinis

Bunce Bikinis is a locally owned boutique bikini shop located in Mission Beach, San Diego. We specialize in fun patterns, comfort, and an athletic fit. The store uses its online platforms and prime location to sell it’s custom bikinis.

Target audience

The target audience for Bunce Bikinis is beach loving girls from ages 15-30. The suits are built to be stylish and cute, but also to not fall off in the waves. The target girl would love to surf and tan, she wouldn’t be limited by her bikini. Our suits are also affordable and created sustainably so the target customer would care about the environment. The people that use our site would be mainly girls that saw our bikinis online through social media and wanted to order them. They could also have heard of our store and wanted to check it out because they aren’t able to get to our location in San Diego.

Their goal is to find a bikini that works for the beach and the ocean with good coverage and stick in place technology, all while being stylish.
